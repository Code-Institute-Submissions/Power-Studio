About our Studio

Power Studio is a studio for all who identify as female. We wanted to create a studio that is a safe place for women without making it overly feminine, and we believe that we achieved our dream.


With a team of fully qualified instructors, with qualifications in pole dancing instruction aswell, you can trust us to help you achieve your fitness goals.


Our studio is fully equipped with everything you need to have a beneficial fitness class. We also have 6 poles from X-Pole that fold away into the ceiling to make room for all our other classes.
